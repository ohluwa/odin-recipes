Ramen

Description

This recipe is better than any make-your-own ramen seasoning recipes. This recipe hits the spot and there is no MSG or artificial ingredients. It will take about 10 minutes to prepare and 10 minutes to cook.

Ingredients

  • 4 cups vegetable broth
  • 4 cups water
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 1 tablespoon ground ginger
  • 1 tablespoon ground Sriracha hot sauce
  • 9 ounces soba noodles

Steps

  1. Combine broth, water, soy sauce, sesame oil, ginger, and hot sauce in a pot.
  2. Bring to a boil.
  3. Add noodles to boiling mixture and cook until noodles are tender, yet firm to the bite.
  4. Transfer noodles to serving bowls and top with desired amount of broth.
